What is XhasaSmall?

XhasaSmall was established in order to help small businesses effectively compete with larger well-established businesses. We provide small businesses access to a wide range of users who use our platform to search for whatever product/services they need. We support small businesses by ensuring that all the products/services on our platform are strictly provided by small businesses.
